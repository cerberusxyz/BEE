Email Etiquette Simulator

How to Play

You are reviewing a simulated corporate email inbox. Your task is to determine whether each email follows proper business email etiquette.

  • Read each email carefully in the inbox.
  • Decide if the email is appropriate or inappropriate.
  • Incorrect answers add strikes (3 strikes = game over).
  • Complete all emails to clear the inbox.
