📧 Email Etiquette Simulator

Corporate Training Overview

Welcome to your email communication training module.

You will review simulated corporate emails and evaluate professionalism.

How to Play:

• Select a difficulty level to begin.

• Open emails from your inbox and evaluate each message.

• Decide if the email follows proper business etiquette:

  • ✅ Professional tone, clarity, structure → Appropriate
  • ❌ Informal, vague, rude, or unclear → Inappropriate

• Each correct answer increases your final score (out of 100).

• Each incorrect answer adds a strike.

• 3 strikes will end the simulation early.

• Your final grade (A–F) is shown at completion.
