📧 Email Etiquette Simulator

Corporate Training Overview

You are being trained to evaluate professional workplace communication.

How to Play

• Open emails from your inbox
• Decide if each message is appropriate or inappropriate
• 3 strikes ends the simulation
• Final score (0–100) and grade (A–F) shown at the end

Tutorial

Step 1: Click an email in the inbox
Step 2: Read the message carefully
Step 3: Choose Appropriate or Inappropriate
Step 4: Review feedback explanation
Step 5: Complete all emails or reach 3 strikes
